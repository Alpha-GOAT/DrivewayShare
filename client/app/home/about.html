About Driveway Share

  • Driveway Share is a way to share your personal parking spot with someone in need. We connect people who are in need of parking in congested areas where parking can be both hard to find and pricey.
  • Go ahead and try it out! Search for listings near an address on the homepage. Click on the icon to display more information.
  • If you would like to post a listing, signin/signup and create a new listing from your profile page.
